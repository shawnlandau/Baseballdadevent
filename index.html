ABOUT THE CHALLENGE

Your Kids Play Here. Now It's Your Turn.

Every weekend you show up. You drive to practices, haul gear, call out encouragement from the bleachers, and live every at-bat through your kid's eyes.

This time, it's YOUR name on the lineup card.

Dads vs. Mano Suave is a Cooperstown-style individual skills competition open exclusively to dads of active 9U–14U travel baseball players — held at The Ripken Experience® Myrtle Beach, one of the finest youth baseball facilities in the country.

  • Open to all dads of 9U–14U travel ball players
  • 5 individual skills events — one competitor at a time
  • Cooperstown-style format
  • Compete to beat Jose "Mano Suave" Polanco's scores
  • Held at The Ripken Experience® Myrtle Beach, SC

Meet Your Competition

Jose "Mano Suave" Polanco is an elite baseball instructor and coach with years of high-level experience on the field. He builds champions — players with strong fundamentals and unbreakable mental toughness.

Before event day, Mano Suave will post his benchmark score for each of the 5 events. Your job? Beat every single one.

THE 5 EVENTS

All individual. All against one benchmark. Just you vs. Mano Suave's score.

EVENT 1

HOME RUN DERBY

12 pitches. You supply your own pitcher. Step in, lock in, and swing for the fences. Most home runs in 12 pitches wins.

GOLDEN ARM
